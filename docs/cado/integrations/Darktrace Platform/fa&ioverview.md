# Forensic Acquisition & Investigation Platform Introduction

The Forensic Acquisition & Investigation (FA&I) platform empowers security teams to investigate and respond at cloud speed. By automating data capture and processing, the platform effortlessly delivers forensic-level detail that help teams investigate cyber security incidents and provide SOC analysts with high fidelity details to support detections from XDR/SIEM tools.
